Education and Experience



I am currently attending Brandeis University majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Economics. My prefered languages are Java and Python but I'm also very familiar with Javascript and Scheme. I enjoy doing web development and have used the ReactJS framework and several front-end CSS frameworks including Bootstrap and Ionic.

At Brandeis my courses have ranged from traditional Data Structures and Algorithms to functional programming classes writing simplfied compilers and evaluators as well as Theory of Computation.

Last summer I worked for MLB Advanced Media as a Front End Developer on their Sponsorship, and Web Platform teams. I was responsible for building up pages for sponsors and working on a custom interactive page for their AWS servers.

Additionally, I worked at OMB Labs where I was responsible for writing the WayWay API, designing their two websites using Strikingly, and for conducting research on potential clients.

My biggest personal project has been creating a interactive base website for Kinetic, an action oriented think tank with chapters at many different colleges and universites. I acted as the lead developer choosing the entire stack and developing all the functionality.
