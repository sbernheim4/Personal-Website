About

Since my childhood, I have always been fascinated with computers and how they work.

In 9th grade I had my first opportunity to start programming in Processing and immidiately I was hooked. This is what I had been waiting for.

I pursued computer science spending two summers at ID Tech Camps where I was taught the basics of C++.

At Brandeis University I am studying Java and Scheme and have taught myself Python. I took summer courses in web development. and continue to learn these languages and more.

In addition to pursuing a BS in Computer Science I am minoring in Economics taking additional classes in Math, Politics and History.
