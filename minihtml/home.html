Education

I am currently a junior at Brandeis University majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Economics. My prefered languages are Java, JavaScript and Python and I'm familiar with C++. I love working on full-stack web development projects and exploring the ever evolving world of web development.

At Brandeis I have taken CS courses in Data Structures and Algorithms, Operating Systems, Functional Programming, Databases, Big Data Analysis and many more. The majority of my classes have been in Java but some have been taught with Scheme and Python.

In Economics I have taken Micro and Macro economic theory as well as classes focusing on specific fields and locations such as Economics of the Middle East.

Before formally studying Computer Science at Brandeis, I took intro classes in High School using Processing, a sort of java derivative, and spent two summers at ID Tech Camp working in C++ and sought out multiple opportunities to deepen my knowledge of Computer Science by practicing what I had learned and reading books and articles.
